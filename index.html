About

This website currently sponsored by the Eastern Iowa IT Sector Board. The Eastern IT Sector Board is the gathering of IT community partners in the Kirkwood Community College Service area to help advance the IT discipline.

Current areas of focus for the Eastern Iowa IT Sector Board include:

  • Providing an audience for collaboration within IT in the Creative Corridor
  • Finding ways to promote IT Career Awareness
  • Using the ideas of the Sector Board to help provide influence to local IT training options.
